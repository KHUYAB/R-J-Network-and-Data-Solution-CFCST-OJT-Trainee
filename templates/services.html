Structured Cabling & Fiber Optics

We design and install high-performance structured cabling systems that serve as the backbone of your digital communication. From Copper (Cat5e, Cat6, Cat6a) to Fiber Optic solutions, we ensure seamless data flow across your organization.

Our team follows TIA/EIA international standards to minimize downtime and maximize bandwidth efficiency. Whether you are setting up a new office or upgrading an existing data center, our precision-engineered cabling ensures your network is future-ready.

Key Solutions:
  • End-to-end Copper and Fiber Optic installation and splicing
  • Data Center design and cable management (Rack & Stack)
  • Fluke Testing and certification for guaranteed performance
  • Cleanup and organization of messy server rooms/IDF cabinets

Managed Network & IT Security

R&J provides enterprise-grade network configuration and security to protect your digital assets. We specialize in building robust Wi-Fi networks and secure VPN connections for businesses of all sizes.

From firewall deployment to active directory setup, we ensure that your internal systems are optimized and protected against unauthorized access. Our proactive monitoring helps detect bottlenecks before they affect your daily operations.

Scope of IT Services:

  • Indoor and Outdoor High-Density Wi-Fi Solutions (Ubiquiti, Cisco, Aruba)

  • 24/7 Managed IT Support and Network Troubleshooting
